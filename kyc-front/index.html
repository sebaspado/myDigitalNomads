How it works

Efficiency meets simplicity with KYC.me streamlined process:

Effortless Document Scanning

OCR Scanner processes the document automatically, extracting and validating key data.

  • 13,000+ document templates covering 247 countries and territories are supported
  • 138+ languages and scripts, including Latin, Cyrillic, Hebrew, Greek, Arabic, Chinese, and more

Unleash Biometric Magic

Biometric data is compared against the validated document, initiating a liveness check for added security.

  • Detects various spoof attacks, including 2D photos, 3D masks, and deepfake videos, with configurable confidence scores for tailored security
  • Fast and simple verification process with on-screen guidance, ensuring quick and easy user compliance with minimal effort.
